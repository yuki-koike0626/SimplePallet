Is there a shortcut key to split screen on Mac?
Not by default, but with SimplePallet you can instantly split screen using Cmd + Arrow keys.
Why is Mac split screen not working?
This is usually caused by insufficient Accessibility permissions. Go to "System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility" and ensure SimplePallet is turned ON. If it is already ON, try turning it OFF and then ON again.
How do I check permissions or set auto-launch?
Click the SimplePallet icon in the menu bar at the top of the screen to check permissions. We also recommend checking "Launch at login" here for convenience.
